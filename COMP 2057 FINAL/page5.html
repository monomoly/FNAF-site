FNAF 4: Nightmare

In the final chapter of the original Five Nights at Freddy's story, you will need to defend yourself once again from the likes of Freddy Fazbear, Chica, Bonnie, Foxy, and other terrifying creatures that hide in the darkness. As an unknown child character, your task is to keep yourself safe until 6am by monitoring the doors and preventing any unwanted visitors from entering your closet or approaching you from behind on the bed. You have only a flashlight to rely on for protection, which can be used to scare away any lurking creatures at a distance. However, you must also exercise caution and listen carefully, as shining the light too close to an approaching creature could result in your demise.


This is personally one of my favourite games of the series in terms of visuals. The animations of the animatronics are bone chilling and so facinating to look at. Scott Cawthon really took creative liberties when creating these versions of the characters and it adds great value to the experience.
