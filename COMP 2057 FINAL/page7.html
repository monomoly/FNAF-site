Easter Eggs

Although I can go on forever with the little easter eggs hidden throughout each game, instead i'd like to cover a few of the most memorable ones.


Game Easter Egg
Five Nights at Freddy's 2 - Freddy nose honk on Toy Freddy's nose, on the left poster.
- Hallucinations
- Skip the night by hovering over Toy Freddy's nose on the left poster and pressing C, D, and +.
- Can also unlock Night 6 and 7 with C, D, and 1 or 2.
Five Nights at Freddy's 4 - Purple Guy can be seen fitting someone into a Spring Bonnie suit in the back room
Ultimate Custom Night - Old man consquences
- put on withered Golden Freddy + death coin = Fredbear jumpscare
FNaF VR: Help Wanted - When collecting all the tapes, Glitchtrap will appear behind you
- Lolbit or Yenndo replacing Funtime Foxy
- Nightmare Endo
- Plushtrap
