Fan Games

Five Nights at Freddy's has inspired hundreds of individuals to create their own spin off fan games. Some of the apps developed are directly based off of the lore and play into a lot of the common easter eggs already established in the series. Other games are known to have their own unique twists that introduce new concepts and alter the orginial story to be something completely different. I cannot name every fan game created but I do want to highlight the more popluar ones that have been documented on over time.

1.One Night At Flumpty's

The game "One Night At Flumpty's" lives up to its title by featuring only one night of gameplay, but with increasing difficulty as the night progresses. The objective is for the player to survive until 6:00 AM by evading Flumpty Bumpty and his friends, including Birthday Boy Blam and Grunkfuss The Clown. As with the "Five Nights At Freddy's" series, failure to defend against these menacing cartoon characters results in a frightening jumpscare for the player.

2. Five Nights at Candy's

Regarded as one of the notable fan games of the Five Nights At Freddy's franchise, "Five Nights At Candy's" was first introduced nearly a year following the revolutionary release of the original game. The plot centers around a newly opened pizza place, which replaces the now-defunct Freddy Fazbear's pizzeria.

3. Aftonbuilt

Aftonbuilt (developed by JeliLiam) is a refreshingly dark take on the events that befall the blaze that destroyed Fazbear's Fright in Five Nights At Freddy's 3. The player will assume the role of a protagonist simply referred to as "The Mechanic," who ominously teams up with the now-deceased founder of the infamous pizzeria franchise, William Afton, to build him a new animatronic body, so he can walk amongst the living once again.
The gameplay of Aftonbuilt is what sets it apart from most Five Nights At Freddy's fan games. Instead of closing doors and simply avoiding or warding off animatronics, players have the ability to fight back and trap the menacing animatronics in Aftonbuilt.
